Because of imbalance between the demand and supply for skills, countries will have skills that are in shortage and skills that are in surplus. Shortage and surpluses of knowledge, skill and abilities are presented in the OECD Skills for Jobs database.
Find out more about skills shortages and surpluses by selecting a country of interest.
Explore the different trees to find out which skills, knowledge and abilities are in shortage or surplus in the selected country. Mouse over a skill (bubble) to find out more details about the skill (in pop-up box), as well as the occupations that contribute most to the shortage or surplus (at the bottom).
